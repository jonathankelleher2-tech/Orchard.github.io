About Our Community

Orchard Pointe is a thoughtfully planned community in which the Homeowners Association’s mission is to provide an enhanced sense of community through transparency, honesty, and activities; to enforce rules with diligence, fairness, and compassion when needed; and to always act with the good of our residents as our primary focus.

  • 144 townhomes with maintained common areas
  • Pavilion & green spaces for neighborhood gatherings
  • Professional property management and active HOA board

Board Meetings

The Board of Directors meets quarterly in January, April, July, and October at the Halfmoon Town Hall from 7:00–8:30 PM. April and October meetings are open to all homeowners. Please submit questions two weeks in advance.

Elections

Board seats are staggered to maintain continuity. Next October election: Secretary, Treasurer, and Member-at-Large. Nomination forms will be distributed in advance.

Committees

Each committee has at least three members, including one Board member. Meeting times are set by the chair and shared via email/text and on Facebook. Minutes are circulated to committee members and the Board.

Property Management

Sentry Management partners with the HOA to handle assessments, vendor contracts (landscaping, snow removal, accounting), compliance support, and homeowner requests. The current contract runs through 2027.

  • • Collect and track homeowner fees & delinquencies
  • • Maintain HOA financial accounts
  • • Manage and certify community vendors
  • • Process Exterior Change Requests
  • • Advise the Board on legal and compliance matters
Sentry Community Portal

Finances

Monthly fees cover snow removal, lawn care, mulching/edging of front beds, mowing of private lawns and common areas, shrub pruning, and retention pond maintenance. Annual financial reviews are shared with homeowners each September.
